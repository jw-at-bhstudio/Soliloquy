# Veritas 2026 App Naming Proposals

## Recommended Direction

- Keep the app name literary, short, and brandable.
- Let the subtitle explain the product function precisely.
- Keep the attachment line fixed for institutional attribution.

## Proposal A

### APP Name

- APP名-英: `Soliloquy`
- APP名-中: `独白`

### APP Subtitle

- APP副标题-英: `A Key Visual Generator for Self-Dialogue`
- APP副标题-中: `「与自我对话」主视觉生成器`

### Additional Information

- 英文附加信息: `Made for Veritas Academy 2026`
- 中文附加信息: `为唯理书院 2026 而作`

### Short Introduction

- 中文 1: 以声音为输入，将语音分析结果转化为可生成、可变形的主视觉结构。
- 中文 2: 它连接声学分析、声纹建模与镜像绘制，把一次“与自我对话”转译为可见图像。
- 中文 3: 生成结果可继续调整、试听与导出，用于年度主题的视觉表达与延展。
- English 1: It turns voice input into a generative visual structure through acoustic analysis and voiceprint reconstruction.
- English 2: It connects signal analysis, voiceprint modeling, and mirrored rendering to visualize the act of self-dialogue.
- English 3: The result can be refined, auditioned, and exported for the key visual system of Veritas Academy 2026.

### Notes

- 气质最准确，文学性强，最贴近“与自我对话”。
- 适合作为正式品牌名、仓库名与页面标题主名。

## Proposal B

### APP Name

- APP名-英: `Monologue`
- APP名-中: `独语`

### APP Subtitle

- APP副标题-英: `A Visual Engine for Self-Dialogue`
- APP副标题-中: `「与自我对话」视觉引擎`

### Additional Information

- 英文附加信息: `Made for Veritas Academy 2026`
- 中文附加信息: `为唯理书院 2026 而作`

### Short Introduction

- 中文 1: 从人声中提取频率、能量与节律，生成可演化的视觉母体。
- 中文 2: 系统支持批量分析、单条细看、参数变形与图形导出，形成完整的生成流程。
- 中文 3: 它不是静态排版工具，而是一套把声音转译为主题视觉的工作台。
- English 1: It extracts frequency, energy, and rhythm from the human voice to produce an evolving visual seed.
- English 2: The system supports batch analysis, focused inspection, parameter deformation, and graphic export in one workflow.
- English 3: Rather than a static layout tool, it works as a studio for translating voice into thematic visuals.

### Notes

- 更直接，更易懂，但文学感略弱于 `Soliloquy`。
- 如果希望名字更大众、更容易第一眼读懂，可选这一组。

## Proposal C

### APP Name

- APP名-英: `Echoform`
- APP名-中: `回形`

### APP Subtitle

- APP副标题-英: `A Voice-to-Visual Generator for Self-Dialogue`
- APP副标题-中: `「与自我对话」声纹视觉生成器`

### Additional Information

- 英文附加信息: `Made for Veritas Academy 2026`
- 中文附加信息: `为唯理书院 2026 而作`

### Short Introduction

- 中文 1: 它把声音留下的波动与回响，转化为可计算、可绘制的视觉形式。
- 中文 2: 从分析工作台到镜像工作台，用户可以完成从采集、生成到导出的完整链路。
- 中文 3: 适合强调“回声”“回应”“自我折返”这一层主题意象。
- English 1: It transforms vocal traces and resonant feedback into a visual form that can be computed and rendered.
- English 2: From analysis workspace to mirror workspace, the app supports the full path from capture to generation and export.
- English 3: This direction is especially suitable when the theme needs a stronger sense of echo, response, and return.

### Notes

- 设计感更强，更像生成系统或视觉工具名。
- 语义偏“回响”而非“独白”，适合作为更当代、更偏设计系统的命名。
